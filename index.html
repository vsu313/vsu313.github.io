Welcome!

Hello! I'm Vanessa (she/her), a third-year Biotechnology student at UC Davis. Currently, I’m working as a student researcher in the labs of Dr. Janine LaSalle and Dr. Ian Korf. My research spans both wet-lab work, where I explore epigenetic factors related to neurodevelopmental disorders, and dry-lab work, where I apply bioinformatics tools to analyze large sequencing datasets to answer biological questions. I’m passionate about both areas and especially enjoy the intersection between them. Outside of my studies, I love hiking, camping, trying new recipes, watching shows, and going on mini adventures with friends.
